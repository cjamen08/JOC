Book Review

Earth Abides, Released in 1949

I read this book during Spring Break a couple years back. Though it's pretty dark, I remember really enjoying reading it. It's a science fiction novel, telling the story of a man who survives a virus which wipes out most other humans on Earth. He ventures around meeting other survivors. It emphisized the importance of civilization and family.

Movie Review

The Lord of the Rings, Dec 19, 2001

I watched the Lord of the Rings movies when I was really young, and recently rewatched them. The first one is my favorite. It's about Frodo Baggins' journey to destroy the one ring. Many friends accompany him in this first film. It's a classic, and I enjoy it every time I watch it.
